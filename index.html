TikTok Content Bot Overview TikTok Content Bot is a content automation tool designed to help creators generate short-form video content ideas and media for TikTok accounts. The app focuses on trend-aware content generation and scheduling support for creators. The application is intended for personal and experimental use by the app owner and is not distributed as a consumer-facing product. Key Features - Automated content idea generation based on trending topics - Text and voice-based content creation - Video composition for short-form platforms - Scheduled content workflows - Creator productivity assistance Intended Use This application is used to: - Assist with content creation workflows - Analyze public trends on TikTok - Generate original media content for posting The app does not provide analytics, account management, or user authentication features. Data & Privacy TikTok Content Bot does not collect, store, or share personal user data. - No user accounts are created - No personal information is stored - No data is shared with third parties - Publicly available trend data may be processed temporarily for content analysis Contact Email: contact@genlocus.dev Website: https://genlocus.github.io/tiktok-content-bot/ Legal Notice This application is developed independently and is not affiliated with or endorsed by TikTok or ByteDance.
